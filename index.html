Origins and Purpose

AeroVista Airlines was founded in 2009 by a coalition of aviation veterans who believed long-haul travel could feel personal, sustainable, and tech-forward. Headquartered in Singapore with operational hubs in Doha, Munich, and Seattle, we started with eight city pairs and a promise: curate every journey as if we were traveling ourselves.

Milestones That Define Us

  • 2009: Commenced operations with the ultra-efficient Dreamliner fleet and 600 dedicated crew members.
  • 2012: Launched the AeroSync platform delivering real-time itinerary management to every passenger.
  • 2014: Debuted SkyLuxe™ suites, earning the Global Travel Innovation Award for reimagined premium cabins.
  • 2017: Achieved carbon-neutral ground operations across all hubs through solar micro-grids and electric ground support equipment.
  • 2019: Introduced SkyBridge cargo division to support humanitarian relief and med-logistics corridors.
  • 2021: Ranked #1 in APAC for customer satisfaction after deploying proactive concierge AI that resolves 92% of queries before take-off.
  • 2023: Expanded our network to 500+ global destinations with 50 partner airlines and seamless interline experiences.
  • Today: Trusted by 10M+ loyal flyers, operating 320 next-gen aircraft, and certified Platinum for safety and reliability by the International Air Transport Council.

What Sets AeroVista Apart

  • Fleet: All aircraft equipped with biometric boarding, HEPA+ air filtration, and predictive maintenance systems.
  • Cabin Experience: SkyLuxe™ suites, Horizon Premium lounges, and interactive entertainment curated in 28 languages.
  • Service Culture: Crew training academy graduates 2,300 ambassadors annually, championing empathy-first hospitality.
  • Smart Operations: AI route optimization and continuous-descent approaches reduce fuel burn by 17% versus industry averages.

Commitment to People & Planet

  • Carbon Goals: Net-zero roadmap for 2040 featuring sustainable aviation fuel partnerships in five continents.
  • Community: AeroBridge Foundation has delivered 1200+ medical evacuation flights and STEM scholarships for 40,000 students.
  • Safety: 15 consecutive years with no major incident, audited quarterly by independent regulators.
  • Innovation: Partnered with NASA and Airbus to pilot hydrogen-hybrid demonstrators beginning 2026.

The Journey Ahead

We believe the future of aviation is immersive, sustainable, and deeply personal. From seamless biometric journeys to personalized inflight wellness programs and real-time language assistance, every initiative is designed to keep you exploring the world with confidence.

Thank you for being part of the AeroVista story—your trust fuels our next horizon.
