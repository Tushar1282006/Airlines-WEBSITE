Flight booking FAQs

Answers to the questions we hear most often from travelers.

Booking & tickets

  • Can I hold a fare? Yes, select “Lock fare” at checkout to hold pricing for 24 hours.
  • How do I add baggage? After selecting your flight, choose “Extras” during checkout or in the Manage Trip portal.
  • Can I travel with pets? Small pets are allowed in-cabin on select routes. Contact support to confirm availability.

Payments & refunds

  • What payment methods are accepted? We support major credit cards, UPI, netbanking, and corporate billing.
  • How are refunds processed? Refunds appear within 7-10 business days after approval.
  • Will I receive an invoice? Yes, invoices are emailed automatically and available for download on the payment confirmation page.

Check-in & travel

  • When does online check-in open? 48 hours prior to departure for most flights.
  • Can I choose seats? Yes, seat selection is available during booking or under Manage Trip.
  • What documents are required? Carry a valid government-issued ID and any visas required by your destination.
