Cancellation & refund guidelines

Review the key timelines and steps to cancel your flight or request a refund.

Flexible fares

  • Cancel within 24 hours of booking for a full refund on all fares.
  • Flexible and Business fares can be changed or refunded up to 2 hours before departure.
  • Promo fares qualify for travel credits; service fees may apply.

How to cancel

  1. Visit Manage Trip and enter your booking reference.
  2. Select the segment(s) you wish to cancel.
  3. Choose refund-to-original payment or travel credit.

Refund processing times
